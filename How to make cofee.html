Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1 to 2 Teaspoons Instant Cofee Powder
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 to 2 Teaspoons Sugar

Preparation steps

  1. Boil half cut of water and pour it to your mug.
  2. Add 1 to 2 teaspoons of instant coffee powder to a mug.
  3. Add a teaspoon of sugar and mix it well.
  4. Add a 1 cup milk and mix it again.
  5. Stir your coffee and serve it.
