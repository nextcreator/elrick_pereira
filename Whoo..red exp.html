Whoa….Why Is My Water Red?

Aim:

To observe what happened when red powder is added to water.

Materials Used:

Red powder,Water,Glass & Spoon.

Procedure:

Take 1/8 teaspoon of red powder and mix with water.Let it sit for few minutes..

Observation:

The red colour mixes with the water making the water red.It changes only the physical property of water, so it’s a physical change.
